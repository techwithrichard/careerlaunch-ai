# Phase 05 · Track 05 — Governance & Analytics Ops

Establish oversight for data, models, and outcomes at organizational scale.

## A. Governance Framework
- [ ] Form cross-functional governance council with clear charter.
- [ ] Schedule regular access reviews and entitlement audits.
- [ ] Maintain policy library (data handling, AI ethics, retention).
- [ ] Track policy exceptions and remediation plans.

## B. Analytics Operations
- [ ] Manage analytics backlog with prioritization aligned to school goals.
- [ ] Implement SLA for analytics requests (dashboards, ad-hoc analysis).
- [ ] Version analytics assets and certify official dashboards.
- [ ] Monitor query performance and optimize warehouse spend.

## C. ML Operations
- [ ] Document model lineage from training data to deployment artifacts.
- [ ] Require approvals for model promotion with reproducibility checks.
- [ ] Automate re-training pipelines with drift triggers.
- [ ] Archive retired models with metadata for compliance.

## D. Business Alignment
- [ ] Tie platform KPIs to school partnership objectives.
- [ ] Publish quarterly impact reports with insights and next steps.
- [ ] Facilitate executive workshops to review roadmap vs outcomes.
- [ ] Integrate governance metrics into leadership scorecards.
