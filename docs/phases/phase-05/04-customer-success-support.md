# Phase 05 · Track 04 — Customer Success & Support

Enable responsive support and measurable adoption for schools and mentors.

## A. Support Operations
- [ ] Integrate ticketing system (Zendesk, Freshdesk) with single sign-on.
- [ ] Define SLAs for incident tiers and monitor adherence.
- [ ] Build knowledge base with context-aware suggestions in-app.
- [ ] Capture root causes from tickets to inform product fixes.

## B. Feedback Loops
- [ ] Launch in-app surveys (CSAT, NPS) tied to key workflows.
- [ ] Provide feedback widgets on dashboards and reports.
- [ ] Escalate negative feedback to account managers automatically.
- [ ] Analyze sentiment trends to prioritize improvements.

## C. Adoption & Training
- [ ] Develop onboarding curriculum for school admins, mentors, learners.
- [ ] Offer live webinars and recorded sessions with Q&A.
- [ ] Track feature adoption via product analytics dashboards.
- [ ] Share quarterly value reports highlighting outcomes achieved.

## D. Account Management
- [ ] Maintain health scoring model (usage, outcomes, feedback).
- [ ] Flag at-risk schools for proactive outreach.
- [ ] Coordinate roadmap previews and early-access programs.
- [ ] Document executive business reviews with agreed actions.
